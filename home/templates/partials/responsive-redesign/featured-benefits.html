Some of the benefits of a mobile-friendly website include:

User experience:

A mobile-friendly website improves the user experience. When your website is easily accessible and readable on any device, visitors are more likely to stay on your site for longer and engage with your content. This can lead to better brand recognition and customer loyalty.

Improved SEO:

Mobile-friendly websites rank higher on search engines. That's because search engines recognize the importance of mobile accessibility, and prioritize mobile-friendly websites in their search results. This means more people will find your website, increasing your visibility and organic traffic.

Increased conversion :

Mobile-friendly websites lead to increased conversion rates. When visitors can easily navigate and use your website on any device, they're more likely to take action and convert into customers. This means more leads and sales for your business.
